About Japan

Japan is a tourist attraction built on extraordinary history and culture. Located on the east coast of Asia, Japan’s beauty is contained within an immense string of islands. The majority of its 125.7 million population is housed on the four main islands of Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku and Kyushu. The capital of Japan is located in Honshu, ranking highly on the world's most populated cities and tourist attractions.

Population: 125.7 million (2021)
Capital City: Tokyo
Spoken Language: Japanese
Prime Minister: Fumio Kishida
Emperor: Naruhito
